Our Story

Journey of Excellence

Established in 1994, Electrotech Enterprises has been at the forefront of instrumentation and automation for 30 years. Starting with maintenance of variable speed drives, we've grown into a trusted integrator serving over 500+ clients across Western Maharashtra, Northern Karnataka, and overseas.

We specialize in sugar and allied industries, offering complete solutions from DCS/PLC systems to online monitoring compliant with CPCB and SPCB norms.

Automation Solutions

Comprehensive automation solutions tailored for sugar and allied industries, delivering efficiency, precision, and reliability

Mill Automation

Transforms sugarcane milling into a high-performance process with automated cane feeding and juice flow stabilization for maximum efficiency.

Boiler Automation

Advanced control and monitoring for boiler houses, ensuring stable operations with intelligent multi-element control.

Falling Film Evaporators

Optimizes evaporation by controlling levels, flows, and emergencies for consistent performance.

Pan Automation

Automates vacuum pans for consistent sugar quality and energy efficiency.

PRDS Automation

Delivers precise steam at required pressure and temperature for plant-wide efficiency.

Condenser Automation

Generates optimal vacuum with minimal water and power usage.

Melter Automation

Precision control for raw sugar melting in refineries.

pH Automation

Ensures precise pH control in juice clarification for crystal-white sugar.

Centrifugal Feed Automation

Accurate feeding to centrifugal machines for optimal separation.

Steam Head Sugar Silo Automation

Optimizes sugar handling from silos to prevent blockages and degradation.
